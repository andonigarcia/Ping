Ping! - What is it? Why do I need it? And how do I benefit?

Traditional email, print, and television advertisments have extremely poor engagement rates. While you might have an enormous amount of impressions, the follow-through per user is atrociously low. Online advertisements are shown to boost this rate through a more personal engagement. Ping! takes this even further. Push notificaitons have the largest engagement rates out of any form of advertising. As such, our interface allows you to tap into your target audience with greater success.

Our platform sidesteps lengthy marketing campaigns and high-profile advertisements. Traditional forms of advertising are very expensive, both financially and time-wise. Our quick 150 character, call-to-action Ping!s, allow you to easily and cheaply push your message out to a large audience.

We have a user-friendly interface that allows you to easily plan out your Ping!s well in advance. Weeks ahead, you can schedule what to say and when to say it so you have more time to focus on what matters most: Your business!

A problem most businesses face, is finding out the effectiveness of their ad campaigns. You never know your impressions, and rarely know your engagements from traditional ads. This is is a major problem that we here at Ping! won't tolerate! We provide extensive and powerful analytics FOR FREE.

We track impression and engagements rates like every other online advertiser. But that's chump change. We are interested in the finer - and more relevant - details. We tell you, how many engagements turn into customers walking through your door. We're working with POS systems to tell you out of those that actually entered, how many purchased an item. You want to know more than just engagement rates, you want to know: Turn-over. Sales. Revenue generated. The true effectiveness of our platform. And we want to show it to you! We want to inform you of which times of day are most effective for your Ping!s, which types of promotions drive the most sales, and all together how to best profit from our product.

While our platform is aimed for hosting deals, we understand that many of our business clients do not hold a myriad of discounts. No worries, our system allows you to push out notifications for any type of promotion. Ping! is a platform to host deals, discounts, and promotions. We just request that you frame it in a way that entices users through a call-to-action. Maybe say something like "It's 5 O'Clock somewhere! Come on down to start your Happy Hour", or "Begin your morning with the perfect latte".
