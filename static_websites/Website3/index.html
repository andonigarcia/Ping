Ping! aims to bring advertising to mobile users the right way. We capitalize on new technologies to bring the most state of the art advertising for both customers and companies. Using geo-fencing and user proximity, we send real-time push notifications to users about advertisements that they are interested in.

On the consumer-side, Ping! utilizes extensive user preferencing to custom tailor notifications about deals, specials, and events. We use highly advanced machine learning algorithms to ensure that users get the most relevant ads for them while they're right near the location. Further, a comprehensive databasing platform provides users with a clean interface to find all the local deals and promotions going on in real-time around them.

On the company-side, Ping! provides three critical services that set us apart from any other modern advertisement distributer. In order to ensure that each business maximizes their ROI, we but powerful analytics first and foremost. From the ground up, we had analytics in our vision. From detailed consumer acquisition statistics to demographic breakdowns, our cutting edge tools will ensure that every store owner knows exactly how their advertising is boosting their sales. Second our direct, real-time notifications mitigate the problem of ensuring consumer impressions. Further, because of the nature of push notifications, we convert these impressions to successful engagements at higher rates than any other form of mobile or online advertising. This, coupled with our user-preferences ensures that each consumer that receives your Ping! is the most likely candidate to follow through and purchase items. Finally, our quick and easy advertising platform sidesteps heavy marketing campaigns and expensive graphic designs, in exchange for a quick 100 character promotions. We know how to target our consumers, but what we know even more than that, is how to help our businesses. Our devotion to our core business services are how we stand out above any other mobile advertisement distributer.
