About Ping!

Ping! brings advertising to mobile users the right way. It is a mobile advertising company that takes advantage of geo-fencing and user proximity to send real-time push notifications to users about relevant ads.

On the user-end, Ping! utilizes extensive user preferences to custom tailor notifications about deals and specials to each consumer. This highly intelligent machine learning algorithm ensures that users get the most relevant ads for them while they're right near the location. Further, a comprehensive databasing platform provides users with a clean interface to find all the local deals and promotions going on in real-time around them.

On the company-side, Ping! provides critical benefits to ensure businesses maximize their return on investment. First and foremost, Ping! has powerful analytic tools. From detailed consumer acquisition statistics to demographic breakdowns, our strong analytics will ensure that every store owner knows exactly how their advertising is boosting their sales. Next, our direct, real-time notifications solve the problem of advertisements hitting their target audience. This coupled with user-preferences ensures that each consumer that receives the Ping! is the most likely candidate to follow through and purchase items. Finally, our quick and easy advertising platform sidesteps heavy marketing campaigns or expensive graphic designers, in exchange for a quick 100 character promotion.
